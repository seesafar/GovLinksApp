About OneLink

OneLink is an independent unified gateway that organizes links to trusted Saudi government platforms in one secure, modern page.

Vision & Purpose

OneLink is designed to solve a simple daily problem: “Where is the correct official link for this service?”

  • Collects the most important Saudi government platforms in one page.
  • Uses only official domains — no random ads, no trackers, no shortcuts.
  • Provides a clean and fast layout that works on mobile and desktop.
  • Helps citizens, residents and visitors reach services with less time and effort.
